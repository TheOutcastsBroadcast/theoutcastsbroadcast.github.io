Definition Of Streaming

Streaming refers to any multi-media content shared to electronic devices by the means of internet connection whereas the features it contains are instant-viewing, instant-playback, and storage-free/friendly.

Types Of Streaming Content

There's a variety of streaming content types and each come with a different purpose: to entertain, to educate, to persuade, and to convert. Examples of streaming content types are television shows, movies, video games, ebooks, and so much more. To sum it up, streaming content types are any material that utilizes multi-medias which incorporates video, sound, pictures or a combination of it all.

Platforms For Streaming

Considering today's generation, technology evolved so much that hardly you can count the number of platforms with your own fingers. Either way, the platforms for streaming are any group of technologies that utilizes multi-media content. For instance are Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitch, Netflix, Spotify, Apple Music (iTunes), Pinterest, Google+, LinkedIn, Discord and such.
